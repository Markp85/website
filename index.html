Latest Match Reports

Northside Rovers vs City United 2 - 2

A thrilling draw at the Riverside Stadium. Northside took an early lead with a stunning volley in the 12th minute, but City United clawed back with a penalty just before halftime. The second half saw end-to-end action, culminating in a 90th-minute equalizer that sent the away fans into a frenzy.

Green Athletic vs The Yellows 1 - 0

It was a defensive masterclass from Green Athletic today. Despite having only 35% possession, they managed to hold off a relentless attack from The Yellows. A classic counter-attack in the 75th minute proved to be the decisive moment, securing three crucial points for the home side.

Dynamo FC vs Spartak West 4 - 3

An absolute goal-fest under the floodlights! Dynamo FC looked comfortable with a 3-0 lead at the break, but complacency set in. Spartak West mounted an incredible comeback to level the score at 3-3, only for Dynamo's star striker to head home the winner in stoppage time.
